Nurture the generosity within each person, and bring the resources for creating change in the world to everyone.
Giving can and should be an enjoyable, uplifting, and personally meaningful experience that leads to the change you want to see in the world. We see a future where this is the norm, not the exception. A future where people continue to practice and grow in their ability to create change because they see the power of giving that feels good and does good over time. And, when a generous action doesn’t create the change that was intended, it’s embraced as an opportunity to learn or try something different the next time.
